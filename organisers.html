After five beautiful years of congresses, exchanges and other EGEA-interactions, I retired in Berlin where I live and work as Data Scientist to this day. In the meantime, I did have some professional flings with geography and hope to one day work closer to my roots again.     I studied Communications BA and only realised how awesome geographers are when starting my Masters in Cultural Geography. In my free time I enjoy doing bootcamp and learning to grow some veggies in my new garden.
